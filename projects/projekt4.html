Der 100 m² große LG-Messestand bot interaktive Erlebnisse mit Mini-Turnieren, Influencer-Battles, Live-Demos und Social-Media-Aktionen. Design und E-Sport-Flair zogen Gamer und LEC-Fans gleichermaßen an.

LG’s 100 m² exhibition booth offered an interactive experience with mini-tournaments, influencer battles, live demos, and social media activations. The design blended tech precision with e-sports energy, attracting both competitive gamers and LEC fans.

Targeted content, influencer integration, and prize promotions strengthened the connection between brand, product, and community, supporting LG’s strategy to become a premium tech partner in the European gaming market.
